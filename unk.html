The name of our club is "SAHAYATRI CLUB". The club was established in the year 2014. We first started our club by worshiping Saraswati. Since then every year our club has been doing this Saraswati Puja. Since then we have not limited ourselves to just Saraswati Puja Our club has been involved in various types of social work. Also, our club gives blankets to poor and needy people every year. So that the poor people get some comfort, and distribute book,pencil,pen,Khata to all the meritorious students of the village. So that all students can study in a better way. We all know that "giving blood is a great gift". For this our club organizes blood donation camp every year, so that we can contribute a little to the welfare of the society.
